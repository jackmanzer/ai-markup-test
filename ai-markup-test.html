      St. Gabriel the Archangel Parish
      No Subtitle

      t. Gabriel the Archangel Parish is a welcoming Catholic community dedicated to worship, service, and spiritual growth. Rooted in faith and guided by the teachings of the Church, the parish seeks to foster a vibrant environment where individuals and families can encounter Christ and grow as disciples.

      Through the celebration of the sacraments, ongoing faith formation, and outreach to those in need, St. Gabriel the Archangel Parish strives to live out the Gospel in everyday life. Parishioners are encouraged to participate fully in the life of the parish and to share their gifts in service to one another and the wider community.

      Whether you are new to the area, returning to the Church, or seeking a spiritual home, St. Gabriel the Archangel Parish welcomes you to join in prayer and community.
