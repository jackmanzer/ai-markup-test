      • Ash Wednesday Masses and Prayer Services
        Ash Wednesday is on Wednesday, February 18, beginning the season of Lent. We are aware that on Ash Wednesday the church is very full, with many desiring coming to receive ashes on their foreheads to symbolize the attitude of internal penance which we are called to throughout Lent. In response to this great outpouring, we will have two Masses as well as five Prayer Services. The Prayer Services are child friendly, and only last about 20 to 30 minutes. Masses will be held at 12 p.m. and 7 p.m. Prayer services will be held at 7:30 a.m., 10 a.m., 4 p.m, 5 p.m. and 6 p.m.

        Dear Parishioners, I wish to inform you that I am retiring as Pastor of St. Catherines in December of this year. A new Pastor will be appointed and he will take up office in January, 2027. I will be retiring to the John Paul II Priests retirement center in Georgetown. With love, Fr. Pat.

        St. Catherines very own Troop 448 is selling Texas Native Hardwood Mulch (brown, non-dyed) for $6 per bag.       • Souper Bowl of Caring 2026
        February 7 - February 8
        Super Bowl Sunday is also the day of Souper Bowl of Caring, where organizations all over the country collect money and food to help alleviate hunger in their communities. The Youth of St. Catherine will collect monetary donations after the weekend Masses on February 7 & 8 to support Meals on Wheels of Central Texas. For information on participating, contact Livia Telles-Rodriguez at [emailprotected] . Click the link to volunteer and/or donate online.

      • World Marriage Day
        Saturday , February 7 , 5:00 PM - 6:30 PM
        Celebrate World Marriage Day on Saturday, February 7, at 5 p.m. Mass with a special blessing! If your 2026 wedding anniversary ends in a 0 or 5-year increment, we invite you to register so that your names can be read at the Mass. You will also receive a certificate by mail, signed by Father Pat! Please register by Monday, February 2. All marriages will be blessed at every Mass on February 7 and 8.

      • Knights of Columbus Annual Super Bowl BBQ
        Sunday , February 8 , 8:30 AM - 10:30 AM
        If you pre-ordered from the Knights of Columbus for their annual Super Bowl BBQ, pick up your order on Sunday, February 8, at the Council 8156 Smoker in St. Catherine of Sienas West Parking Lot between 8:30 - 10:30 a.m. This event supports the charities benefitting from our efforts. Want to learn more about the Knights of Columbus? Go to Knights.net! Learn how the Knights of Columbus lead with our faith to protect the family, serve others and defend our values. The benefits of being a Knight are numerous.

      • Women @ the Well Bible Study: The Holy Spirit in the Bible
        Tuesday , February 10 , 9:15 AM - 10:45 AM
        Women of the parish, have you ever wanted to learn more about the third person of the Trinity? Do you wish you had a closer relationship with the Holy Spirit? If so, then join us on Tuesday mornings from 9:15 to 10:45, beginning February 3, as we journey together through the Bible Study The Holy Spirit in the Bible. We will learn about topics like The Holy Spirit in the Beginning, The Holy Spirit and Jesus, and The Holy Spirit at the End of Time. Cost is $20 for the book. Click the link for more information and to register.
